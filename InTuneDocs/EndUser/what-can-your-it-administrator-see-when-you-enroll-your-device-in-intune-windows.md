# What can your IT administrator see when you enroll your device in Intune?

When  you enroll your Windows device in Intune, you are giving your IT administrator permission to manage your device to help protect the company information on the device.

**What IT cannot see**

-   Call and Web history

-	Location

-   Email and text messages

-   Contacts

-	Passwords

-   Calendar

-   Camera roll

**What IT can see**

-   Model

-   Serial number

-   Operating system

-   App names

-   Owner

-   Device name

-   Manufacturer


If you have questions and can't find your IT administrator's contact information, see if it's listed on the [Company Portal website](http://portal.manage.microsoft.com).
